Who I Am

Hi, I’m Noah Baty — a hardworking and motivated Year 12 student at John McGlashan College in Dunedin. I study English, Digital Technology, Math, History, Biology, and PE, and I’ve built a strong passion for understanding how things work.

Over the past two years, I’ve been running my part-time business Tech Talk, providing PC repair, troubleshooting, and custom builds to clients across the Otago Peninsula.

My goal is to help people feel confident with their devices — not just by fixing problems, but by helping them understand their technology better.
